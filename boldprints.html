PATTERNS ELEVATE ANY CLASSIC SILHOUETTE.


Bold prints are more wearable than you think, any classic silohette is elevated when coated in a colorful, big, bold pattern. These outfits are all about balance when you’re first starting out, but if you have an eye for color and pattern mixing and are more comfortable with your expression, its all about maximalism. Both approaches work but the latter takes more consideration, experience and finesse.

A bold pattern is typically the focal point of an outfit. A loud pant may be paired with a white tee, denim cargos with a colorful jacket, a boldly printed dress with minimal accessories. Bold prints aren’t always saturated; neutral tones can form captivating geometric patterns. They can then be paired with saturated accessories to balance the attention of viewers. An ansemble of bold prints can be purposefully and aesthetically overwhelming, althoguh this is a difficult task to complete.
