Since its conception in 1999 in a cramped Brooklyn loft, AVANTE has taken the world by storm. This cutting edge brand originated as the brainchild of Carl Dean and Opal Marks, two college dorpouts, and evolved into the epitome of quality, individuality and, of course, style.

Our style tells a story about who we are and who we want to be. It’s a part of our growing identites. The way we clothe ourselves changes the way we are percieved and even the way we feel.
Your style should be a reflection of who you are, it’s an outlet for authentic expression. Here at AVANTE, we believe style is a way of life. Its about being different, its about the impact your purchase has on others, its about quality over quantity.
